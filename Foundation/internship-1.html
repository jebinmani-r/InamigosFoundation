ABOUT US :

InAmigos Foundation, founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO), is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization committed to creating lasting social impact.

Our Credentials & Recognitions:

  • Registered under Section 8 – Licensed by the Central Government
  • 80G & 12A Certified
  • CSR-1 Registered
  • NITI Aayog Registered
  • IAF ISO 9001:2015 Certified

OUR PROJECTS

Project SEVA:

Provides food, clothes and essential support to needy people.

Project Bachpanshala:

Supports education for underprivileged children.

Project Udaan:

Empowers women through education and skill development.

Project Jeev:

Supports animal welfare and stray animal care.

Project Prakriti:

Promotes environmental sustainability through tree plantation.

Project Vikas:

Provides internships and professional development opportunities.

PURPOSE OF THE PROJECTS

Bachpanshala : To provide education and learning support for underprivileged children.
Seva : To help needy people with food, clothes, and basic necessities.
Jeev : To protect and care for stray and abandoned animals.
Udaan : To empower women through skills and awareness programs.
Prakriti : To protect the environment through tree planting and sustainability activities.
Vikas : To improve students' skills and employability through internships and training.
